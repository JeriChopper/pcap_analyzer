    Basic Principles of the Algorithm
    The Hey Tuya AI Assistant provides intelligent dialogue and voice interaction services based on generative artificial intelligence technology. Users can converse with Hey Tuya via text or voice through the App or compatible smart hardware devices. The system utilizes natural language understanding (NLU) and generation models to identify user intent based on user input, question type (such as IoT device control, feature usage, scene settings, etc.), and knowledge base information.     Algorithm Operating Mechanism
    Hey Tuya identifies user intent and generates corresponding responses by integrating third-party Large Language Models (such as ChatGPT). The system features built-in content safety and compliance detection mechanisms that filter and review generated results in real-time to prevent the output of illegal, harmful, or misleading content.
    Algorithm Application Scenarios
    The Hey Tuya AI Assistant is applied in the App and selected smart hardware devices to provide users with services such as AI device control, AI energy saving, AI to-do lists, AI security protection, and device usage consultation. For example, users can quickly control smart home devices or obtain relevant usage help through natural language interaction. This system is not used for automated decision-making or to produce legally binding acts; AI outputs serve solely as auxiliary information.
    Algorithm Purpose and Design Intent
    The Hey Tuya AI Assistant aims to provide users with functions such as home device control, energy management, security protection, and Q&A chat, helping users utilize the Tuya App more conveniently. Hey Tuya adheres to the principles of "human-centric, transparent, controllable, and responsible AI." When necessary, human support or review channels are provided to ensure that AI outputs do not solely affect user rights or decision-making outcomes.
